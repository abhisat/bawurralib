About Us

Bawurra Foundation is a not-for-profit organisation helping improve literacy ability in Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander children. We want to celebrate Indigenous culture and make it a source of pride.

Bawurra


“Bawurra means Red Kangaroo in the Gamilaraay language. It symbolizes leadership and strength and is an important totem in our shared Indigenous culture.”

Jesse Slok, Co-Founder
